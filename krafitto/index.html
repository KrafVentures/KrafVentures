About program

Krafitto is a summer engagement program to help preschools in engaging kids at home through various resource material, physical assignments, assessments and lots of fun activity sheets. The program is divided into 3 parts

1

Krafitto App

An iOS/ android mobile and tablet app for school with school’s logo and name. App has preloaded content for the program in the form of resource material, assignments and assessments

2

Activity Sheets

A pack of 100+ activity sheets and set of art and craft material home delivered to kids to engage them in joyful activities

3

Family weekend

Unpublished videos, live sessions on fitness, art and craft, story telling and much more for kids and parents to make their weekend a family weekend

Duration of the proram

Krafitto is a 6 week program

    Registration and pre program procedure

    School Registration

  • School need to fill a form to register for the program to make their school name visible in child registration form
  • After successful registration of the school, a Krafitto app download link, Krafitto code and Krafitto login id and password shall be provided to school.

    • An online link will be shared with schools which schools need to send to parents for registering students, please ensure parents select the correct school while filling the form.

    Child Registration

  • A child registration link shall be provided to school which school needs to send to parents for registering their child for the program. The registration link will have school name along with Krafitto code, please ensure parents select the correct school while filling the form.
  • Once the parent registers for the program by making full payment, Krafitto shall dispatch the kit and will add the child name in the app
  • A confirmation mail along with login credentials for the Krafitto app, getlitt app shall be send to registered mail id within 2 days from the date of payment.

    • Weekly schedule

    Every Sunday Krafitto will share the schedule for the coming week, which schools in turn will share with parents. Weekly schedule will comprise of brief introduction to the topic and learning outcomes.

    Live sessions

    Every week their will be minimum 1 live session followed by Q&A round. With the growing demand we many look forward to increase the number of sessions. Post session assignments based on sessions will be shared with schools which in turn they will share with kids and parents. These assignments can be collaborative activities where in kids and parents both are required to participate.

    Content Delivery/ Assessments/ Assignments

    The content will be delivered in below manner everyday

    Week 1

    Krafitto will share the lesson plan for the week containing topics to be covered and details about live session

    Day 1 -

  • Step 1 - Krafitto will share a link with school which will have resource material on the said topic along with assessments and assignments
  • Step 2 - Kids will have to go through the resource material to complete the assessment which is an online questionnaire available at the end of the same link
  • Step 3 - With the related material kids will be asked to do certain physical activity which is to be considered as assignments. These assignments can be videos or photos which with the help of parents will share with school on school’s WhatsApp group or any other communication channel school using.

    • The same pattern will be followed every week and Saturdays will be live sessions days.

    Grading

    Grading system of the whole program is based on assessments and assignments. Whenever the kids complete the assessment the system will record their marks. For every assignment school need to award grades to kids. Every assignment submitted on the same day will attract 10 points, and 5 points when submitted on next day. Schools will be given a report sheet with student names mentioned in it, school need to points against every student weekly.

    Mandatory instructions

    Below are some mandatory instructions for the schools who are willing to register for the program

  • Minimum 50% of the total strength of you school should participate
  • A dedicated resource person from school need to be assigned for day to day interaction with Krafitto, kids and parents
  • As its a program for the school, hence school need to show active participation
